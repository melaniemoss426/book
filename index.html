About the Book

A call to stand and keep the light of faith burning in our nation

Melanie blends biblical insight from the story of Israel and Isaiah, drawing inspiration from the prayers, cries, and warnings of the prophets — presenting a compelling vision of prayer for the nation.

Leaning on the rich Christian heritage of England and the United Kingdom, she shows how remembering God's past interventions strengthens our faith for the present and the future.

This timely book will inspire you to pray with fresh confidence, grounded in Scripture and God's unchanging promises, believing God has not finished with England or the United Kingdom.

Let us be the ones who stand, who pray and who keep the light of faith burning in our nation.
